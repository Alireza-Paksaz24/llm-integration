 Major Economic Summit Focuses on Post-Pandemic Recovery

London, UK — The World Economic Forum held its annual meeting focusing on strategies for economic recovery following the COVID-19 pandemic. Delegates discussed measures to boost employment, support small businesses, and address global supply chain disruptions.

Discussions included the importance of digital transformation and investment in sustainable industries to create resilient economies. Policymakers also explored ways to reduce income inequality exacerbated by the pandemic.

Economist Sarah Nguyen noted, “Recovery is not just about returning to pre-pandemic levels but building a more inclusive and sustainable global economy.”
